  Sports

Badminton

Cambridge University Badminton Club is a place for players to take their game to a higher level. With teams playing in both the local Cambridge league and the national BUCS league, there is plenty of opportunity for players to test themselves. Currently the squad trains three times a week at the University Sports Centre, building physical, technical and tactical abilities towards the big events of the season.

Swimming

Cambridge University Swimming Club consists of two teams which represent Cambridge University in swimming.The swimmers are split into the first and second teams, who train separately but are all entitled to swim in competitions and trial for the Varsity Match.

Asian University Championships in India, China and Japan. Both teams have taken bronze medals at the SCS Championships and the ADAM Student Cup on more than one occasion.

Volleyball

Cambridge University Volleyball Club is one of the most successful sports clubs in the University.In the past, the Men's and Women's Blues have consistently ranked amongst the best Universities in the country and have represented Great Britain at the European University Championships in Serbia, Greece and Slovenia.Both teams have taken silver medals at the BUCS Championships and the EVA Student Cup on more than one occasion.

Rowing

Cambridge University Rowing Club established in 1828 the Cambridge University Boat Club is a club with a rich history of success and an extremely proud heritage, and it is a great honour and priviledge to have been elected as President for the 2017 Boat Race campaign.

I am looking forward with enthusiasm to leading a strong squad of dedicated and committed athletes to prepare for the 163rd instalment of this historic boat race.Currently the squad trains three times a week.
